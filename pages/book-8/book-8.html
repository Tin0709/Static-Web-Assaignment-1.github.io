The Fault in Our Stars


"The Fault in Our Stars" by John Green is a heart-wrenching and beautifully written novel that follows Hazel Grace Lancaster, a sixteen-year-old cancer patient. When she meets Augustus Waters, another young cancer survivor, their lives intertwine in a story of love, loss, and the profound impact of shared experiences. The novel delves into themes of mortality, resilience, and the search for meaning, offering a poignant and honest exploration of life's complexities.

  • Author: John Green
  • Genre: Young Adult, Romance
  • Publication Year: 2012
