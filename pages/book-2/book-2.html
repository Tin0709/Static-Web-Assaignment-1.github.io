Percy Jackson and the Olympians: The Lightning Thief


"Percy Jackson and the Olympians: The Lightning Thief" by Rick Riordan follows Percy Jackson, a young demigod and the son of Poseidon, as he embarks on a quest to prevent a war among the Greek gods. With his friends, Percy navigates through mythological challenges, uncovering his true heritage and discovering his unique powers. Set in a modern world intertwined with ancient mythology, the novel is a captivating blend of adventure, humor, and heroism as Percy faces divine challenges and battles ancient foes.

  • Author: Rick Riordan
  • Genre: Fantasy, Young Adult
  • Publication Year: 2005
